Steak

Description

Quick way to make delicious yummy and juicy steak!

Ingredients

  • steak
  • olive oil
  • butter
  • garlic
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste

Steps

  1. smear olive oil into the steak
  2. put salt and pepper onto both sides of steak and rub it in
  3. heat oil in frying pan over high heat
  4. put in steak and let it cook 3 min on each side
  5. add in butter and garlic and baste
  6. flip steak and baste until desired rarity
